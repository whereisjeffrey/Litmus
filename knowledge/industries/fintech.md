# Fintech Evaluation Playbook

## Trust & Security

### Are security certifications prominently displayed?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** SSL badges, bank-level encryption mentions, SOC 2, PCI DSS badges near sensitive areas
**Why it matters:** 81% of consumers say trust is a deciding factor in financial services. No visible security = no signups.
**Research:** Edelman Trust Barometer: 81% say trust is a deciding factor. Blue Fountain Media: security badges increase conversion up to 42%.

### Is regulatory compliance visible?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** FDIC, SEC, FINRA, state licensing information displayed
**Why it matters:** Regulated industry — missing compliance info raises red flags and may violate disclosure requirements.
**Research:** Compliance is legally required for most financial products. Non-disclosure can result in fines and loss of licensure.

### Is there a physical address and contact info?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Office address, phone number, email visible on site
**Why it matters:** For financial products, anonymity = scam. Physical presence builds trust.
**Research:** Stanford Web Credibility Project: 75% judge credibility by design. Contact info is a top trust signal.

### Is the privacy/data handling policy prominent?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Privacy policy link, data encryption mentions, how user data is handled
**Why it matters:** Financial data is the most sensitive. Customers need to know their data is protected.
**Research:** PwC: 85% of consumers will not do business with a company if they have concerns about its data practices.

## Complexity Reduction

### Are financial terms explained in plain language?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Jargon usage, tooltips for complex terms, plain-language descriptions
**Why it matters:** Financial jargon intimidates and confuses — confused people don't sign up.
**Research:** NN/g: financial sites with plain-language explanations see 30% higher task completion rates.

### Is there a calculator or estimator tool?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Mortgage calculator, loan estimator, investment projector, savings calculator
**Why it matters:** Calculators turn abstract financial products into personal, concrete numbers.
**Research:** HubSpot: interactive calculators increase time-on-site by 3x and conversion by 25%.

### Are fees transparent and clearly structured?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Fee disclosure, pricing tables, no hidden charges language
**Why it matters:** Hidden fees are the #1 complaint in fintech. Transparency builds trust and reduces churn.
**Research:** Forbes: 56% of consumers have left a financial provider due to hidden fees.

### Is the process explained step by step?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** "How it works" section with numbered steps, timeline expectations
**Why it matters:** Financial processes are intimidating. Step-by-step breakdowns reduce anxiety and increase starts.
**Research:** Baymard: process visibility (showing steps) reduces form abandonment by up to 30%.

### Is comparison to alternatives clear?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Comparison tables, "vs traditional banking" content, rate comparisons
**Why it matters:** Financial switching costs feel high. Clear advantages over alternatives motivate the switch.
**Research:** McKinsey: 45% of banking customers are willing to switch for better digital experience.

## Onboarding & KYC

### Is the KYC/verification process streamlined?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Number of verification steps, ID upload flow, time-to-complete estimates
**Why it matters:** KYC abandonment rates are 40-50% for complex flows. Every extra step loses customers.
**Research:** Signicat: 40-50% of potential customers abandon complex KYC processes.

### Can you open an account on mobile?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Mobile-optimized account opening flow, camera integration for ID scanning
**Why it matters:** 73% of consumers prefer digital banking. If mobile account opening is broken, you're losing 73% of your market.
**Research:** J.D. Power: 73% prefer digital banking. 41% switch banks due to poor digital UX.

### Is there a progress indicator during onboarding?
**Tag:** [ENG]
**What to check:** Step counter, progress bar during multi-step account setup
**Why it matters:** Not knowing how long signup takes causes abandonment.
**Research:** NN/g: progress indicators reduce perceived wait time and increase completion rates.

### Are document upload instructions clear?
**Tag:** [ENG]
**What to check:** Accepted file types, size limits, photo quality requirements for ID verification
**Why it matters:** Failed uploads cause re-tries and frustration. Clear instructions prevent this.
**Research:** Signicat: poor upload UX is a leading cause of KYC abandonment.

## Financial Tools & Features

### Are dashboards showing actionable data?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Key metrics prominent, actionable insights, not just data display
**Why it matters:** Data without context is noise. Actionable insights drive engagement and retention.
**Research:** Mixpanel: users who engage with personalized dashboards have 2.5x higher retention.

### Is there real-time or near-real-time data?
**Tag:** [ENG]
**What to check:** Last updated timestamps, real-time balance/portfolio updates
**Why it matters:** Stale financial data erodes trust immediately.
**Research:** Users expect financial data to be current. Delays cause anxiety and support tickets.

### Are notifications configurable?
**Tag:** [ENG]
**What to check:** Alert settings for transactions, balance thresholds, market changes
**Why it matters:** Customizable alerts drive engagement and make the product indispensable.
**Research:** Plaid: push notifications increase daily active usage by 20-35% in fintech apps.

## Mobile Experience

### Does the app feel native on mobile?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Touch interactions, swipe gestures, bottom navigation, native feel
**Why it matters:** Fintech users manage money on the go. A clunky mobile experience drives them to competitors.
**Research:** J.D. Power: mobile banking satisfaction directly correlates with overall bank satisfaction.

### Is biometric login supported?
**Tag:** [ENG]
**What to check:** Face ID, Touch ID, fingerprint authentication
**Why it matters:** Password entry on mobile for financial apps is friction. Biometrics are expected.
**Research:** Visa: 65% of consumers prefer biometric authentication over passwords for financial services.

### Are sensitive actions confirmed with extra verification?
**Tag:** [ENG]
**What to check:** Two-factor for transfers, confirmation screens for large transactions
**Why it matters:** Security without friction — critical for financial trust.
**Research:** Industry standard: PSD2 (EU) requires strong customer authentication for payments.

## Accessibility

### Is the site accessible to elderly users?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Font sizes, contrast ratios, simple navigation, readable financial data
**Why it matters:** Older adults control significant wealth. If they can't use your site, you're excluding your wealthiest segment.
**Research:** AARP: adults 50+ control 83% of US household wealth. Inaccessible financial sites lose this demographic entirely.

### Are error messages clear and non-technical?
**Tag:** [ENG]
**What to check:** Error message language for failed transactions, validation errors
**Why it matters:** Unclear errors in financial contexts cause panic. Clear messages maintain trust.
**Research:** NN/g: clear error messages reduce user anxiety by 50% in high-stakes contexts.
