# E-Commerce Evaluation Playbook

## Product Discovery & Search

### Is site search prominent and functional?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Search bar visibility, placement, autocomplete suggestions
**Why it matters:** Search users are 1.8x more likely to convert — hiding search loses your highest-intent buyers.
**Research:** eConsultancy: site search users convert at 1.8x the rate of non-search users.

### Are product categories logical and navigable?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Category structure depth, naming clarity, number of top-level categories
**Why it matters:** If shoppers can't find products within 3 clicks, they leave for a competitor.
**Research:** Baymard Institute: 50% of e-commerce sites have category navigation issues that directly impact sales.

### Are filters useful and comprehensive?
**Tag:** [ENG]
**What to check:** Filter by price, size, color, rating, availability, brand
**Why it matters:** Effective filters reduce time-to-purchase by 50%.
**Research:** Baymard Institute: 42% of major e-commerce sites lack adequate filtering.

### Is there a "best sellers" or "most popular" section?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Social proof in product discovery — popular items, trending products
**Why it matters:** Best seller sections leverage social proof and reduce decision fatigue.
**Research:** Amazon attributes 35% of revenue to recommendation and best-seller features.

## Product Pages

### Are there enough product images?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Image count per product (benchmark: 5+ with multiple angles)
**Why it matters:** Online shoppers can't touch the product — photos are their only way to evaluate quality.
**Research:** Shopify: product pages with 5+ images see 40% higher conversion than single-image pages.

### Is there zoom and/or video?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Image zoom capability, product demo video
**Why it matters:** Zoom simulates the in-store "pick it up and look at it" experience that builds purchase confidence.
**Research:** eBay: listings with video see 65% more time on page and higher conversion.

### Is the price prominent and clear?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Price visibility, font size, position relative to product title
**Why it matters:** If customers have to hunt for the price, they assume the worst and leave.
**Research:** Nielsen Norman Group: price is the #1 information users look for on product pages.

### Is Add to Cart above the fold?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Add to Cart button position, visibility without scrolling
**Why it matters:** Every scroll required before the buy button reduces impulse purchases.
**Research:** Baymard Institute: the Add to Cart button should be within the initial viewport on both desktop and mobile.

### Are reviews visible and filterable?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Review section presence, star ratings, review count, filter by rating
**Why it matters:** 93% of consumers say reviews influence their purchase decisions.
**Research:** Spiegel Research Center: products with reviews are 270% more likely to be purchased than those without.

### Is stock/availability status shown?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** In stock/out of stock indicator, low stock warnings
**Why it matters:** Hiding availability wastes time and creates frustration when items are unavailable at checkout.
**Research:** Baymard Institute: 32% of users cite "product unavailable" as a key frustration.

### Is return policy visible on the product page?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Return/exchange policy link or summary near Add to Cart
**Why it matters:** Visible return policies reduce purchase anxiety and cart abandonment.
**Research:** UPS/comScore: 67% of shoppers check the return policy before purchasing. Visible return policies reduce abandonment by 17%.

### Are variant selectors intuitive?
**Tag:** [ENG]
**What to check:** Size, color, and option selection UX — dropdowns vs swatches
**Why it matters:** Confusing variant selection leads to wrong orders and returns.
**Research:** Baymard Institute: 21% of sites have poor variant selection UX.

## Cart & Checkout

### Is guest checkout available?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Option to purchase without creating an account
**Why it matters:** Forcing account creation is the #2 reason for cart abandonment after price.
**Research:** Baymard Institute: 24% of users abandon carts when forced to create an account. Guest checkout increases conversion by 45%.

### How many form fields in checkout?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Total field count across all checkout steps (benchmark: 7 or fewer)
**Why it matters:** Every unnecessary field increases friction and abandonment.
**Research:** Baymard Institute: average checkout has 14.88 form fields — the optimal number is 7. Top-performing checkouts have 6-8.

### Are shipping costs shown before the final step?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Shipping cost visibility throughout checkout, not just at the end
**Why it matters:** Surprise shipping costs are the #1 reason people abandon carts.
**Research:** Baymard Institute: 48% of users abandon due to extra costs (shipping, tax, fees) being too high or shown too late.

### Is express checkout available?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Apple Pay, Google Pay, Shop Pay, PayPal Express at top of checkout
**Why it matters:** Express checkout reduces the entire form to one click for returning customers.
**Research:** Shopify: merchants with Shop Pay see 1.72x higher conversion than standard checkout.

### Is the order summary always visible?
**Tag:** [ENG]
**What to check:** Cart contents, pricing, and totals visible throughout checkout
**Why it matters:** Hiding the order summary makes customers second-guess their purchase.
**Research:** Baymard Institute: always-visible order summary reduces "I need to review my cart" abandonment.

### Does the form have inline validation?
**Tag:** [ENG]
**What to check:** Real-time field validation as user types, not just on submit
**Why it matters:** Inline validation reduces errors by 22% and speeds completion by 31%.
**Research:** Nielsen Norman Group: inline validation vs after-submit validation study (2009).

## Trust & Security

### Are trust badges displayed near the payment form?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** SSL badges, payment security icons, Norton/McAfee seals near card entry
**Why it matters:** Trust badges near payment fields reduce the anxiety spike that causes last-second abandonment.
**Research:** Blue Fountain Media: trust badges increase conversion by up to 42%.

### Are multiple payment methods shown?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Visa/MC/Amex logos, PayPal, Apple Pay icons visible early
**Why it matters:** 6% of users abandon because their preferred payment method isn't available.
**Research:** Baymard Institute: 6% of cart abandonment is due to lack of payment options.

### Is there a money-back guarantee?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Guarantee badge or text near CTA/checkout
**Why it matters:** Guarantees remove the last barrier to purchase — fear of making a mistake.
**Research:** VWO: money-back guarantee badges increase conversion by 15-20% in A/B tests.

## Mobile Shopping Experience

### Can you complete a purchase on mobile?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Full checkout flow functionality on mobile, no broken steps
**Why it matters:** Mobile is 73% of e-commerce traffic but converts at half the desktop rate — mobile UX is the gap.
**Research:** Statista: mobile commerce accounts for 73% of all e-commerce but converts at roughly half the desktop rate.

### Is Add to Cart sticky on mobile?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Fixed/sticky Add to Cart button on mobile product pages
**Why it matters:** On mobile, users scroll long product pages — if the buy button scrolls away, impulse purchases die.
**Research:** Baymard Institute: sticky Add to Cart on mobile increases add-to-cart rates significantly.

### Are product images swipeable on mobile?
**Tag:** [ENG]
**What to check:** Touch/swipe gesture support for product image galleries
**Why it matters:** Mobile users expect swipe — tap-to-advance feels broken.
**Research:** Google: mobile users expect native gesture support. Sites without it feel "broken."

### Are form inputs sized for thumbs?
**Tag:** [ENG]
**What to check:** Input field height (44px minimum), tap target spacing
**Why it matters:** Small inputs cause mis-taps and frustration, especially during checkout.
**Research:** Apple HIG: minimum 44x44px touch targets. Google: 48x48px recommended.

## Returns & Support

### Is the return policy easy to find?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Return policy link in footer, product pages, and checkout
**Why it matters:** 67% of shoppers check return policy before buying — not finding it kills sales.
**Research:** UPS/comScore: 67% check returns before purchase. Narvar: 96% would shop again if returns are easy.

### Is live chat or help easily accessible?
**Tag:** [ENG]
**What to check:** Chat widget, help link, phone number visibility
**Why it matters:** Customers with questions who can't get help immediately will buy from a competitor who helps them.
**Research:** Forrester: 53% of US online adults abandon purchases if they can't find quick answers.

### Is shipping information clear before checkout?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Shipping costs, delivery timeframes, free shipping thresholds visible on product pages
**Why it matters:** Unknown shipping terms are the #1 reason for cart abandonment.
**Research:** Baymard Institute: 48% abandon due to unexpected extra costs. 22% abandon due to slow delivery times.
