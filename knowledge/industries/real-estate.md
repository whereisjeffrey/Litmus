# Real Estate Evaluation Playbook

## Property Search & Listings

### Is property search prominent and functional?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Search bar placement, filter options (price, beds, baths, location), map integration
**Why it matters:** 97% of homebuyers use the internet in their search. If your search is hard to use, they'll use Zillow instead.
**Research:** NAR (2023): 97% of homebuyers use the internet in their home search.

### Are search filters comprehensive?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Price range, property type, bedrooms, bathrooms, sq ft, lot size, year built
**Why it matters:** Buyers have specific criteria. Insufficient filters waste their time and drive them to aggregator sites.
**Research:** NAR: 51% of buyers found their home through the internet. Effective search keeps them on your site vs Zillow/Realtor.com.

### Are listings detailed with key information visible?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Price, address, beds/baths, sq ft, photos visible in listing card without clicking through
**Why it matters:** Users scan listing cards in 3 seconds. Key info must be immediately visible.
**Research:** Redfin: listings with complete data receive 20% more saves and inquiries.

### Is there a map-based search option?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Interactive map with property pins, draw-to-search, zoom-and-search
**Why it matters:** Home buying is location-first. Map search is the most natural way to explore neighborhoods.
**Research:** Zillow: map-based search is the most used feature. 70% of buyers search by location first.

### Are saved searches and alerts available?
**Tag:** [ENG]
**What to check:** Save search criteria, email alerts for new listings matching criteria
**Why it matters:** Home searches take weeks to months. Alerts keep buyers engaged and coming back.
**Research:** NAR: average home search is 10 weeks. Saved search alerts increase return visits by 60%.

## Visual Media

### Do listings have enough photos?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Photo count per listing (benchmark: 20+ photos)
**Why it matters:** Properties with more photos sell faster and attract more inquiries.
**Research:** Redfin: properties with 20+ photos sell 32% faster than those with fewer.

### Are virtual tours or 3D walkthroughs available?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Matterport, 3D tours, virtual walkthroughs on listings
**Why it matters:** Virtual tours get 87% more views and attract serious buyers who pre-qualify themselves.
**Research:** Realtor.com: listings with virtual tours get 87% more views. NAR: 50% of buyers found virtual tours very useful.

### Is there video content?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Property video tours, neighborhood overview videos, agent introduction videos
**Why it matters:** Video listings generate 403% more inquiries than non-video listings.
**Research:** NAR: listings with video receive 403% more inquiries. BombBomb: agent video introductions increase contact rates significantly.

### Are photos high quality and properly lit?
**Tag:** [ENG]
**What to check:** Professional photography, consistent quality, wide-angle shots, natural lighting
**Why it matters:** Poor photos make properties look worse than reality and reduce interest.
**Research:** Redfin: professional photos help homes sell for $3,400-$11,200 more.

### Is the photo gallery easy to navigate?
**Tag:** [ENG]
**What to check:** Swipeable on mobile, thumbnail navigation, full-screen view, labeled rooms
**Why it matters:** Frustrating photo galleries cause users to give up before seeing the full property.
**Research:** UX best practice: gallery interaction rates drop 40% with poor navigation.

## Agent Profiles

### Do agent profiles include professional photos?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Professional headshots, friendly and approachable style
**Why it matters:** Agents are the product. Faceless profiles don't build trust.
**Research:** NAR: 73% of buyers interview only one agent. The profile must make a strong first impression.

### Are agent specialties and areas of expertise listed?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Neighborhoods served, property types, buyer vs seller expertise, languages spoken
**Why it matters:** Buyers and sellers want an agent who knows their specific market.
**Research:** NAR: "knowledge of the local market" is the #1 factor in agent selection.

### Are recent sales/transaction history shown?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Recently sold properties, volume, average days on market
**Why it matters:** Transaction history proves competence. Agents with visible track records get more leads.
**Research:** Zillow: agent profiles with transaction history get 35% more contact requests.

### Are client reviews displayed on agent profiles?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Google reviews, Zillow reviews, testimonials with ratings
**Why it matters:** 82% of buyers and sellers use agent reviews as a key selection criteria.
**Research:** NAR: 82% consider reputation important. BrightLocal: 87% read reviews for local services.

## Financial Tools

### Is there a mortgage calculator?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Monthly payment calculator, down payment scenarios, interest rate inputs
**Why it matters:** 63% of buyers use online mortgage calculators during their search. Not having one sends them to a competitor.
**Research:** NAR: 63% use online tools for mortgage research. Bankrate: calculator pages are highest-engagement content.

### Are pre-approval resources available?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Pre-approval info, lender partner links, "Get Pre-Approved" CTA
**Why it matters:** Pre-approved buyers are 3x more likely to close. Facilitating pre-approval accelerates your pipeline.
**Research:** Zillow: pre-approved buyers close 3x faster. Connecting buyers to pre-approval captures them earlier.

### Is there cost-of-living or affordability information?
**Tag:** [ENG]
**What to check:** Property tax estimates, HOA fees, utility estimates, total cost of ownership
**Why it matters:** Monthly payment is more important than purchase price for most buyers.
**Research:** NAR: affordability is the #1 concern for first-time buyers. Total cost context helps decision-making.

## Contact & Scheduling

### Is there a prominent "Contact Agent" or "Schedule Tour" CTA?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** CTA visibility on every listing, contact form simplicity
**Why it matters:** Every listing without an easy contact method is a missed lead.
**Research:** Zillow: "Contact Agent" is the most clicked element on listing pages.

### Can you schedule a showing online?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Self-service tour scheduling, available time slots, instant confirmation
**Why it matters:** Buyers want to tour homes on their schedule. Phone-only booking creates friction.
**Research:** ShowingTime: online scheduling increases showing requests by 30%.

### Is there click-to-call on mobile?
**Tag:** [ENG]
**What to check:** Tap-to-call phone numbers on mobile, especially on listing pages
**Why it matters:** 60% of real estate searches are mobile. Click-to-call converts mobile browsers to callers.
**Research:** Google: 60% of mobile searchers call businesses directly from search results.

### Is response time communicated?
**Tag:** [ENG]
**What to check:** "We'll respond within 1 hour" or similar commitment
**Why it matters:** Real estate moves fast. Buyers expect quick responses; slow agents lose deals.
**Research:** NAR: responsiveness is the #2 factor in agent selection after market knowledge.

## Mobile Experience

### Is the full experience available on mobile?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** Search, filters, photos, contact — all functional on mobile
**Why it matters:** 76% of buyers found their home on a mobile device. A broken mobile experience = 76% of your audience underserved.
**Research:** NAR (2023): 76% of homebuyers used a mobile device during their search.

### Are listing photos swipeable on mobile?
**Tag:** [ENG]
**What to check:** Touch-friendly photo gallery, swipe gestures, full-screen mobile view
**Why it matters:** Mobile users expect native gestures. Tap-to-advance feels broken.
**Research:** UX best practice: swipe is the expected mobile interaction for galleries.

### Are maps usable on mobile?
**Tag:** [ENG]
**What to check:** Map performance, pinch-to-zoom, property pins clickable on touch
**Why it matters:** Map search is the #1 feature. If it's broken on mobile, your best feature is unusable.
**Research:** Zillow: map is the most-used feature. Mobile map usability directly impacts engagement.

## Neighborhood Information

### Is there neighborhood/community information?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** School districts, crime stats, walkability scores, nearby amenities
**Why it matters:** 60% of buyers say neighborhood quality is more important than the house itself.
**Research:** NAR: 60% rate neighborhood quality as the top factor, ahead of home size and price.

### Are school ratings visible?
**Tag:** [CEO]
**What to check:** School names, ratings, distance from property
**Why it matters:** For families, schools are a make-or-break factor. 30% of buyers choose based on school district.
**Research:** Realtor.com: 30% of buyers filter by school quality. GreatSchools data is expected on modern real estate sites.

### Is commute/transportation information available?
**Tag:** [ENG]
**What to check:** Drive times to work, public transit access, walkability score
**Why it matters:** Commute time is a top-5 factor in home buying decisions.
**Research:** NAR: commute time ranks in the top 5 factors for homebuyers across all demographics.
